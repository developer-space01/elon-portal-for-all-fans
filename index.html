Elon Musk: Architect of Tomorrow

Born: June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa

Education: University of Pennsylvania (Physics & Economics)

Net Worth: $350B+ (as of 2026)

Elon Reeve Musk is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at SpaceX; early investor, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of the Musk Foundation. In 2025, he became the first person to send a civilian crew into orbit.

His vision: A self-sustaining city on Mars, sustainable energy worldwide, and safe artificial intelligence.
